# MIDAS — Company Overview

> MIDAS is a Global Conversation Intelligence platform for financial services.

## Context

MIDAS was born from the direct observation of friction in financial product distribution. Advisors worldwide spend over 70% of their time on administrative tasks rather than selling. They operate with a primitive tech stack (personal WhatsApp, spreadsheets, physical notebooks) where valuable data is lost in unrecorded conversations.

## What is MIDAS

MIDAS is a **Global Conversation Intelligence** platform designed for financial advisors. It transforms organic conversations into structured financial assets through three core pillars:

1. **Zero-Behavior Change**: Passive capture of conversations (WhatsApp and calls) without forcing the advisor to learn new systems or change their workflow.
2. **AI Intent Detection**: Real-time identification of financial needs and credit intent within informal chats.
3. **Automated Application Generation**: Transforming 45 minutes of manual data entry into a 2-minute review process.

## Mission

To become the infrastructure layer for financial intent globally, turning every professional conversation into a processed credit application without any friction for the advisor.

## Core Philosophy

- **Zero-Behavior Change**: We meet the advisor where they already work. If the tool requires a new habit, it's a failure.
- **Instant Value (Aha Moment)**: Our goal is to deliver the first pre-filled credit application within **24 hours** of setup.
- **Augmentation, Not Replacement**: MIDAS empowers the human advisor by removing the administrative "black hole".

## Business Model

**Performance-based**, aligning incentives with the advisor's success:

- **Free for the Advisor**: Removes the cost barrier and drives viral adoption.
- **Lender/Originator Fee**: We charge the financial institution for every qualified, pre-filled application sent.

## Market & Strategy

While MIDAS has a global vision, our initial validation and scaling are concentrated in high-growth markets:

| Region | Key Insight |
|------|-----------|
| **Global** | $1.2T+ in loan applications processed annually with significant drop-offs due to manual friction. |
| **LatAm (Initial Focus)** | WhatsApp penetration >90%. High growth in digital credit adoption (42% YoY in WA Business API in Colombia). |

**Initial Verticals**: Mortgages and Auto Loans — highest transaction value, most painful documentation process, and existing team expertise (1,000+ advisors network).

## Moat

- **Zero-Friction Technology**: Proprietary approach to passive capture on encrypted channels.
- **Distribution Network**: Immediate access to 1,000+ active advisors and history of 80,000+ processed products.
- **Domain Expertise**: Team with deep background in financial operations and lending infrastructure.
