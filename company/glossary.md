# MIDAS Glossary

> Core definitions and domain terms used across the MIDAS documentation.

## Context

MIDAS operates at the intersection of Finance, Conversation Intelligence, and AI. This glossary ensures both humans and AI agents use consistent terminology.

## Core Concepts

### Conversation Intelligence
A software category that analyzes professional conversations to extract actionable insights. While players like Gong and Chorus dominate the US market (pricing ~$250/user/month), MIDAS is the global solution optimized for asynchronous, mobile-first channels like WhatsApp.

### Zero-Behavior Change (Zero-Friction)
Our foundational design principle. We believe that if a tool requires a new habit, it's a failure. MIDAS works invisibly within the tools advisors already use (WhatsApp and calls), ensuring a 96%+ adoption rate.

### Passive Capture
The automatic extraction of conversational data without manual entry. Our browser-native technology (Chrome Extension) reads conversations from WhatsApp Web, ensuring explicit consent and zero friction for the advisor.

### Intent Detection
AI-driven analysis of organic conversations to identify signals of financial need (e.g., "I'm looking for a house", "I need to finance a car"). MIDAS classifies these signals into specific credit products.

### The "Aha Moment"
The first time an advisor receives a fully pre-filled credit application from a real conversation. Our goal is to deliver this within **24 hours** of setup, proving immediate value.

### Adoption Fatigue
The phenomenon where users abandon tools that require separate logins or workflow changes. This is the primary reason why traditional CRMs fail for independent advisors.

## Domain & Ecosystem Terms

### Financial Advisor (Independent)
Professional contractors who sell financial products (mortgages, auto loans, insurance) independently. Their client list IS their business. They primarily use personal WhatsApp to communicate with clients globally.

### Pre-filled Application
A credit application document automatically populated with data extracted from conversations. It transforms 45 minutes of paperwork into a 2-minute review.

### Consent Mechanism
A mandatory disclosure to the client before AI capture begins. Example: "This conversation may be processed by my AI assistant to speed up your application." This is a key trust and compliance feature.

### Lender / Originator
The financial institution (bank, fintech, credit union) that issues the loan. They pay MIDAS for every qualified, pre-filled application received (Performance-based model).

### Regional Equivalents (Examples)
- **Correspondente Bancário**: The Brazilian term for independent advisors distributing banking products (e.g., Franq Open Finance, Teddy Open Finance).
- **Mortgage Broker**: The standard term in the US/UK markets for independent credit intermediaries.

### BSP (Business Solution Provider)
Meta-certified providers for WhatsApp Business API access (e.g., Treble.ai, Blip). While MIDAS uses a browser-native approach for zero friction, we integrate with BSPs for downstream workflows.
